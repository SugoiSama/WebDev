Biography

Shradha has an MA in Psychology and has been working as a licensed therapist for over 7 years. She provides both individual and group therapy sessions, focusing on promoting mental health awareness and support across Nepal.

Outside of her clinical practice, Shradha is also a frequent speaker at mental health workshops and conferences. She works closely with NGOs to make mental health services more accessible to the general population.
